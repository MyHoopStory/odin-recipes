Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

This is directions for an all-time great lunch, PB&J

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape Jelly

Steps

  1. Place two slices of bread out and grab the peanut butter, jelly, and a knife
  2. Use the knife to spread the peanut butter on one slice and then jelly on the other. Cut as wanted, then serve
